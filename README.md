Delivery Driver Profit Calculator (UK)

A simple but powerful desktop app for UK delivery drivers to understand their real earnings after tax and costs.

This tool is designed for people working in courier, van delivery and gig-economy jobs (Amazon DSP, DPD, Evri, Uber Eats, Royal Mail etc.), where income often looks higher on paper than what you actually keep.

Purpose of the App

It helps you see exactly:

=How much you earn per day / week / year
=What you actually take home after tax (PAYE or self-employed)
=How much your van, fuel and expenses reduce your profit
=Whether a job is actually worth it or not

Instead of guessing, you get a real breakdown of your income.

** Key Features**

Full profit calculator (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly)
UK tax estimation (2025/26 rules)
PAYE and self-employed support
Job profiles (save different delivery companies)
Scenario comparison (good / realistic / worst case)
Cost breakdown (van, fuel, insurance, etc.)
Visual charts for income vs expenses
Export results to Excel or CSV

 Installation
1. Install Python

Make sure Python 3.10 or higher is installed:
https://python.org

2. Install dependencies
pip install PySide6 matplotlib pandas openpyxl
3. Run the app
python main.py
 How to use it
Open the app
Add your job details (day rate, days, expenses)
Choose employment type (PAYE or self-employed)
Click Calculate
Check your real net income
Compare different job scenarios if needed
Export results if you want to keep records
   UK Tax Info (2025/26)

The app uses approximate UK tax rules:

Personal Allowance: £12,570
Income Tax: 20% / 40%
National Insurance included (PAYE & self-employed rules)
Minimum Wage reference: £12.21/hour

Note: All tax calculations are estimates and not official advice.



Saved job profiles are stored locally on your device.
